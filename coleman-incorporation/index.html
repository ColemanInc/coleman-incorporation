About Us

Transforming the Real Estate Market

At Coleman Incorporation, we are committed to shaping the future of real estate, blockchain, and technology. Our vision is to revolutionise the property market by making investment more accessible, liquid, and affordable for all.

Through innovative tokenised real estate solutions, we aim to break down traditional barriers and create a new era where property ownership is democratised and fully tradable on the blockchain.

Breaking Barriers for Working-Class Investors

One of our key goals is to remove the obstacles that have traditionally excluded working-class individuals from investing in real estate, one of the most valuable asset classes. By lowering the barriers to entry, we are enabling people to invest in property with whatever they can afford, providing access to a historically exclusive market.

Investing in Crypto Adoption

Beyond real estate, we are investing in critical infrastructure for mass crypto adoption. Our focus on Know Your Customer (KYC) and on-ramping solutions ensures that cryptocurrency becomes accessible and secure for everyone.

Skin Project: Innovation in Skincare

Coleman Incorporation is also committed to diversification. Our Skin Project represents our move into advanced skincare solutions. Combining cutting-edge technology with deep industry expertise, the Skin Project is set to transform how we approach skincare, offering innovative solutions that meet modern needs.

By investing in disruptive technologies across these industries, Coleman Incorporation is leading the way towards a more innovative, inclusive, and connected future.
